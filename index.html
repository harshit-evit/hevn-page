Problem & Opportunity

Limited EV Charger Network

A lack of readily available charging stations in key locations, especially along highways and in rural areas, making long-distance travel in EVs challenging.

Access Issues

Limited access to charging stations discourages EV adoption, creating a barrier to the growth of electric mobility in the region.

Lack of Facilities

There are no wayside amenities available along with EV Chargers on Highways, making charging stops inconvenient for travelers.

About Us

HEVN is a subsidiary of EVI Technologies (An IIT Delhi alumina company). At HEVN, we develop Electric Vehicle (EV) charging stations at various locations in Himachal Pradesh by integrating renewable energy solutions along with wayside facilities.

Our mission is to create a comprehensive network of charging infrastructure to support the rapidly growing EV market in India, with a special focus on the unique geographical challenges of Himachal Pradesh.
